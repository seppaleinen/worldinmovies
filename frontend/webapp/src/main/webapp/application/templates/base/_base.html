Imdb ratings

To upload your rated movies from imdb follow these steps

  1. Make sure that you've logged into your imdb-account
  2. Click on your user ratings menu
  3. Copy the id after /user/ in the url:
  4. Click the download button
  5. Choose the downloaded file named ratings.csv
  6. Click the upload button
